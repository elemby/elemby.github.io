About Me

Hello, my name is Luke Barber. I am a recent graduate from the University of South Florida, where I obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Mathematics. In my studies I focused on Mechatronic and Bioelectronic Systems.

In additon to my academic studies, I held the USF IEEE Robotics and Hardware Chair for two terms. In this role I helped fellow students learn the basics of mechatronic control hardware.

In my spare time, I play the Great Highland Bagpipes and the Scottish Smallpipes. I am currently constructing a set of Uilleann Pipes and a set of Northumbrian Pipes.
